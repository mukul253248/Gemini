Gemini is a family of highly capable, multimodal AI models developed by Google DeepMind, designed to understand and operate across text, code, audio, image, and video. Formerly known as Bard, it powers Google's chatbot, app, and AI features across Workspace and home devices, offering advanced reasoning and creativity.
